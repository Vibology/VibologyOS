   - Sephira: Geburah
   - HD Center: Heart (Willpower/Ego)
   - Function: Strength, judgment, burning away the useless

6. **Virtues** - Melechim (Kings)
   - Sephira: Tiphareth
   - HD Center: Solar Plexus (Spirit/Emotion)

#### THIRD TRIAD (The Messengers - Human Affairs)
7. **Principalities** - Elohim (Gods)
   - Sephira: Netzach
   - HD Center: Spleen (Intuition/Immune)

   - Sephira: Hod
   - HD Center: Sacral (Generative Life Force)

9. **Angels** - Kerubim (The Strong)
   - Sephira: Yesod
   - HD Center: Root (Pressure/Adrenaline)
