## VIII. Risks & Cautions

### Cultural Appropriation
**Risk:** Indigenous and non-Western traditions require extreme care
**Mitigation:**
- Always cite sources, respect intellectual property
- Acknowledge when knowledge is closed/sacred
- Avoid "spiritual tourism" or superficial treatment
- Consult Indigenous scholars when possible
- Use comparative analysis (patterns) not extraction (stealing)
