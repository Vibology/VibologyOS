## Core Domain

The Window is a **64-card oracle deck** that translates the timeless 64-fold architecture (I-Ching hexagrams / Human Design gates / Gene Keys) into contemporary 1980s cultural encoding.(Blueprint 2026; Huang 1998; Rudd 2013; Ra Uru Hu 2011) It provides a divination system grounded in ancient wisdom while speaking through modern archetypal imagery—pop culture icons, catalytic life events, and parallel domains of human experience.(Blueprint 2026)

The Window bridges **[[Human Design]]'s mechanical precision**, **[[I-Ching]]'s wisdom**, **[[Gene Keys]]' transformative arc**, and **[[Personal Mythos]]'s mythic resonance** into a coherent oracle system accessible to contemporary consciousness.

The Window's architecture consists of **three parallel categorical domains**—not hierarchical stages but equal categorical realms where cards exist:

### 3. The Six Houses  - The Domains

**6 parallel domains** of human experience governing the 42 House cards:

**Elemental Trinity (Immediate Experience):**
- **House of the Land** (Earth) - Physical/grounded, practical stability
- **House of the Sea** (Water) - Emotional, feelings, relational depth
- **House of the Flame** (Fire) - Vital/instinctual, action, movement

- **House of the Chronicle** (Narrative) - Metanarrative, virtues shaping meaning across time
- **House of the Spirit** (Consciousness) - Soul health, inner divinity, psychospiritual integrity
- **House of the Stars** (Mastery) - Material excellence, achievement, personal mastery

Each House contains 7 cards mapping specific gates to that domain's spectrum of experience (positive virtues ↔ shadow expressions).
