# The Four Watchtowers
*The Great Table of Earth — Elemental Tablets of Divine and Angelic Names*

---
## Essence
The **Four Watchtowers** (also called the **Great Table** or **Tablets of the Earth**) constitute the architectural core of [[Enochian Tradition|Enochian magic]]. Revealed to [[John Dee and Edward Kelley]] between 1583-1584, these four elemental tablets form a unified grid containing:

- **Divine names** (Names of God governing each element)
- **Angelic hierarchies** (Kings, Seniors, and lesser angels)
- **Magical squares** (Sub-angles, Kerubic, Servient, and Calvary Cross positions)
- **Directional attributions** (East/Air, South/Fire, West/Water, North/Earth)

Each Watchtower is a **12x13 grid of letters** from which practitioners derive names of power for elemental invocation, manifestation work, and cosmological exploration. Together with the **Tablet of Union** (which unifies all four), they create the most sophisticated system of elemental magic in the Western esoteric tradition.

## Structure of the Great Table

### Overall Configuration

The Great Table consists of:

**Four Elemental Tablets** — Each 12 columns × 13 rows = 156 squares per tablet
**Tablet of Union** — A central cross of 5 rows × 4 columns = 20 squares
**Total Letters** — (156 × 4) + 20 = 644 letters

### The Tablet of Union

**Position:** Occupies the center where the four elemental tablets meet (forming a cross)

**Function:** Contains the **unifying divine names** that must be invoked before working with any individual Watchtower

**Structure:**
```
E X A R P
H C O M A
N A N T A
B I T O M
```

**Divine Names (read vertically):**
- **EXARP** — Air (East)
- **HCOMA** — Water (West)
- **NANTA** — Earth (North)
- **BITOM** — Fire (South)

**Invocation:** These names are vibrated before any Watchtower work to establish unity and balance across the four elements.[^1]

## The Structure of Each Watchtower

Every Watchtower tablet (12×13 grid) contains the following components:

### 1. The Calvary Cross (Linea Spiritus Sancti)

**Position:** The central vertical column (6th column) and horizontal row (6th row), forming a cross

**Function:** Contains the **divine names** ruling the entire Watchtower

**Names Derived:**
- **Vertical name** (13 letters) — Read from top to bottom
- **Horizontal name** (12 letters) — Read from left to right
- **Sub-names** — Various combinations of letters from the cross

**Example (Air Tablet):**
- Vertical: **ORO IBAH AOZPI** (13 letters)
- Horizontal: **RZIONR NRZFM** (12 letters)

### 2. The Four Sub-Angles (Lesser Angles)

**Position:** The four quadrants created by dividing each Watchtower at the Calvary Cross

**Structure:** Each sub-angle is a 5×6 grid (30 squares)

**Function:** Each sub-angle governs:
- **10° of a zodiacal sign**
- **A sub-element** (e.g., Fire of Air, Water of Fire)
- **A Tarot Court Card** (King, Queen, Prince, Princess)

**Total Sub-Angles:** 4 tablets × 4 sub-angles = 16 sub-angles (corresponding to the 16 Court Cards of the Tarot)

### 3. The Kerubic Squares (Four Corners)

**Position:** The four corner squares of each sub-angle

**Function:** Govern the **Kerubic forces** (the four living creatures of Ezekiel's vision: Lion, Eagle, Bull, Human)

**Attribution:**
- **Top-left** — Air element of the sub-angle
- **Top-right** — Water element of the sub-angle
- **Bottom-left** — Fire element of the sub-angle
- **Bottom-right** — Earth element of the sub-angle

### 4. The Servient Squares

**Position:** The remaining 26 letters in each sub-angle (after removing the 4 Kerubic corners)

**Function:** Provide names of **lesser angelic and demonic entities** who serve the higher forces

**Derivation:** Names are extracted by various methods (diagonals, spirals, specific patterns)

### 5. The Six Seniors

**Position:** Immediately surrounding the Calvary Cross in each Watchtower

**Function:** Six planetary angels governing the Watchtower, one for each classical planet (excluding one planet per element)

**Planetary Exclusions:**
- **Air** excludes Jupiter
- **Water** excludes Mercury
- **Fire** excludes Luna (Moon)
- **Earth** excludes Venus

**Derivation:** Seven-letter names read from specific positions around the Calvary Cross

### 6. The King

**Position:** The central square where the vertical and horizontal arms of the Calvary Cross intersect

**Function:** The supreme ruling angel of the entire Watchtower

**Name Length:** Eight letters (derived from surrounding squares)

## Deriving Angelic Names

### Method for Extracting Names

The names of angels are derived from the Watchtowers using specific methods:

**1. The Six Seniors (7-letter names):**
- Read from the squares immediately adjacent to the Calvary Cross
- Each Senior governs a planetary force within the element

**2. The King (8-letter name):**
- Derived from the central square and surrounding positions
- Some traditions use the central letter plus spiraling outward

**3. Kerubic Angels (varies):**
- The four corner letters of each sub-angle
- Sometimes combined with adjacent letters for fuller names

**4. Servient Angels (3-7 letters):**
- Extracted from diagonals, rows, columns within sub-angles
- Golden Dawn methods use complex geometric patterns

### Example: Deriving a Senior Name (Air Tablet, Mars)

**HABIORO** (Mars in Air):
- Read horizontally from the right side of the Calvary Cross, top row
- H-A-B-I-O-R-O

**Invocation:**
- "HABIORO, Senior of Mars in the Watchtower of Air, come forth..."

## Invocation
### Opening the Watchtowers (Full Ritual)

**1. Preparation:**
- Banishing ritual (LBRP or similar)
- Purification of space and self
- Set clear intention for the working

**2. Invoke the Tablet of Union:**
Vibrate the unifying names:
> *"EXARP, HCOMA, NANTA, BITOM — Let the four elements be in balance and harmony."*

**3. Invoke the Four Watchtowers (in order: East, South, West, North):**

**Example (Air Watchtower):**
> *"In the East, I call upon BATAIVAH, great King of Air!*
> *I invoke the Six Seniors: HABIORO, AAOZAIF, HTMORDA, AHAOZPI, AVTOTAR, HIPOTGA.*
> *Let the winds of wisdom blow, let thought be clarified, let communication flow.*
> *I stand at the threshold of the Eastern Watchtower. Open the gates!"*

**4. Specific Work:**
- Petition for knowledge, power, or vision
- Scrying into the elemental realms
- Invoking specific angels for particular purposes

**5. Closing:**
- Thank the Kings and Seniors
- Dismiss with respect: *"Go in peace, return to your stations."*
- Banishing ritual (LBRP)

## Fallen Aspect

### Dangers of Elemental Imbalance

**Overinvoking Air:**
- Mental overstimulation, anxiety, disconnection from body
- "Paralysis by analysis," intellectual arrogance

**Overinvoking Water:**
- Emotional overwhelm, boundary dissolution, psychic flooding
- Depression, escapism, victim consciousness

**Overinvoking Fire:**
- Burnout, aggression, recklessness
- Ego inflation, domination, destructive rage

**Overinvoking Earth:**
- Stagnation, rigidity, materialism
- Fear-based control, hoarding, inertia

**Solution:** Always invoke the **Tablet of Union** first to maintain balance. Close with banishing to release excess elemental force.

### Servient Entities (Demonic Forces)

Not all entities in the Watchtowers are benevolent. The **Servient Squares** contain names of **demonic** or **chaotic** forces who serve the higher angels but can cause harm if invoked improperly.

**Precautions:**
- Never invoke Servient names without full knowledge of their function
- Always work under the authority of the King and Seniors
- Use protective circles, pentagrams, and divine names

### Vibology Synthesis

**All cross-system correspondences, modern interpretations, and ethical frameworks represent Vibology synthesis, not original Dee/Kelley doctrine:**

- **Qabalistic Four Worlds correspondence** (Air/Atziluth, Water/Briah, Fire/Yetzirah, Earth/Assiah) — Golden Dawn Qabalistic overlay, not Enochian
- **Human Design center correspondences** (Air/Throat, Water/Solar Plexus, Fire/Sacral, Earth/Root) — Modern synthesis bridging HD mechanical system with elemental magic

- **Zodiacal sub-angle attributions** (each sub-angle = 10° of specific zodiac sign) — Golden Dawn astrological synthesis
- **Shadow work on elemental imbalance** (overinvoking Air/anxiety, Water/overwhelm, Fire/burnout, Earth/stagnation) — Modern psychological framework
- **Ethical considerations** ("never invoke Servient names without full knowledge," "always work under authority of King and Seniors") — Contemporary magical ethics and safety culture
- **Practical applications** (communication magic, emotional healing, will/power work, grounding/stability) — Modern ceremonial magic interpretations

**Servient entities clarification:** The distinction between "benevolent" higher angels and "demonic/chaotic" Servient forces is a Golden Dawn interpretation. Dee's original doctrine presented all entities in the tablets as part of the angelic hierarchy, with Servient squares simply containing lesser angels serving higher authorities.

**Planetary exclusion theology:** The doctrine that each element naturally excludes one planet (Air/Jupiter, Water/Mercury, Fire/Moon, Earth/Venus) is elegant but not explicitly explained in Dee's manuscripts. It may be a later Golden Dawn rationalization based on observed tablet structure.

**LBRP integration:** The practice of opening Watchtower rituals with the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (invoking Raphael, Michael, Gabriel, Uriel) is a Golden Dawn innovation. Dee did not use the LBRP—his protective methods involved prayer, the Sigillum Dei Aemeth, and invocation of divine names.
