## Overview

**Angels of Fire** is a broad category referring to angelic beings associated with the element of **fire**—purification, transformation, divine wrath, and illumination. The term encompasses several specific orders and beings:

### Primary Associations

1. **[[Seraphim]]** — The "Burning Ones" (from Hebrew *saraph*, "to burn"), the highest angelic order, who surround the throne crying "Holy, Holy, Holy" (Isaiah 6:2-3)

2. **[[Ashim]] / [[Ishim]]** — "Souls of Fire" or "Flames," the order at Malkuth composed of fire and snow

3. **[[Michael]]** — The Archangel of the Sun and Fire, guardian of the South in the LBRP

4. **[[Uriel]]** — Whose name means "Fire of God" or "Light of God"

### Elemental Function

Fire in angelic symbolism represents:
- **Purification** — Burning away impurity
- **Transformation** — The alchemical flame
- **Divine Presence** — The burning bush, the pillar of fire
- **Judgment** — The refiner's fire
