## Overview

The **Sephiroth** (singular: Sephira) are the ten emanations or attributes through which Ein Sof (the Infinite) reveals itself and creates the physical and metaphysical realms. They are the "vessels" that receive and transmit divine light, forming the structure of the [[The Tree of Life]].

The word "Sephiroth" derives from the Hebrew root ספר (saphar), meaning "to count" or "to tell"—the Sephiroth are the numbers through which God "counts" creation into being.

## The Ten Sephiroth

### 1. Kether (Crown) — כתר
**Position:** Top of Tree | **Planet:** Primum Mobile | **Archangel:** [[Metatron]]
The first emanation—pure being, divine will, the point before extension. Kether is unknowable; it is the source.

### 2. Chokmah (Wisdom) — חכמה
**Position:** Top Right | **Planet:** Zodiac/Stars | **Archangel:** [[Raziel]]
The first movement—pure force, the Father, active creative energy without form.

### 3. Binah (Understanding) — בינה
**Position:** Top Left | **Planet:** Saturn | **Archangel:** [[Tzaphkiel]]
The first form—the Mother, receptive understanding that gives shape to Chokmah's force.

### 4. Chesed (Mercy) — חסד
**Position:** Right | **Planet:** Jupiter | **Archangel:** [[Tzadkiel]]
Expansive love, benevolence, the generous king who gives without measure.

### 5. Geburah (Strength/Severity) — גבורה
**Position:** Left | **Planet:** Mars | **Archangel:** [[Kamael]]
Restrictive power, judgment, the warrior who cuts away what must be removed.

### 6. Tiphareth (Beauty) — תפארת
**Position:** Center | **Planet:** Sun | **Archangel:** [[Michael]]/[[Raphael]]
The heart of the Tree—harmony, the reconciler of opposites, the Christ center.

### 7. Netzach (Victory) — נצח
**Position:** Lower Right | **Planet:** Venus | **Archangel:** [[Haniel]]
Emotion, desire, art, nature—the triumph of creativity and feeling.

### 8. Hod (Splendor) — הוד
**Position:** Lower Left | **Planet:** Mercury | **Archangel:** [[Michael]]/[[Raphael]]
Intellect, communication, magic—the splendor of thought and language.

### 9. Yesod (Foundation) — יסוד
**Position:** Lower Center | **Planet:** Moon | **Archangel:** [[Gabriel]]
The astral realm, dreams, the unconscious—the foundation upon which Malkuth rests.

### 10. Malkuth (Kingdom) — מלכות
**Position:** Bottom | **Planet:** Earth | **Archangel:** [[Sandalphon]]
Physical reality, the body, the manifest world—the Kingdom where all above crystallizes.
