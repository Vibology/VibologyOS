# Practitioner Protocols
*Weekly, Monthly, and Quarterly Audits for The Magdalene Path*

---
## Purpose

**This document is FOR YOU—the practitioner walking The Magdalene Path.**

Unlike [[Practices and the Lunar Cycle]] (which provides daily/lunar rhythms for anyone on the path) and [[Contemplative Prayer]] (which teaches silent prayer methods), **this file contains protocols specific to YOUR journey**—tracking where you are on [[The Eight Boughs of Ascent]], auditing your kenosis practice, assessing relational dynamics, and integrating with your Human Design mechanics.

**These protocols are PRIVATE PRACTICE.** Clients never see this. This is your spiritual GPS, your developmental dashboard, your honest self-assessment tool.

**Why formal protocols?**
- **Without tracking, you drift.** You can be "on the path" for years without actual ascent.
- **The ego lies.** It will tell you you're farther along than you are (inflation) or farther behind than you are (false humility).
- **Integration requires awareness.** You cannot integrate what you don't notice.
- **Rhythmic check-ins create accountability.** Weekly/monthly/quarterly beats ensure you don't bypass necessary work.

**The structure:**
1. **Weekly Bough Check-In** (10 minutes) — Where am I on the vertical axis?
2. **Monthly Soul Work Audit** (30-45 minutes) — How is my kenosis/singleness/relational practice?
3. **Quarterly Shadow Scan** (1-2 hours) — What am I bypassing? Where is the false self hiding?
4. **Relational Practice Protocols** (ongoing, if in partnership) — Fifth Way specific check-ins
5. **HD Mechanics Integration** (ongoing) — How does your specific design inform your path?

## Weekly Bough Check-In

**Frequency:** Every Sunday evening (or your designated "Sabbath")
**Duration:** 10 minutes
**Method:** Journaling or silent contemplation

**The practice:**

### Step 1: Locate Yourself (5 minutes)

**Read the descriptions of [[The Eight Boughs of Ascent]] and ask:**

**Where am I RIGHT NOW on the ascent?**

| **Bough** | **Stage** | **Markers** | **Am I here?** |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **First** | Foundation | Wrestling with judgment and wrath; learning compassion | ☐ |
| **Second** | Mind | Confronting ignorance and intolerance; seeking wisdom | ☐ |
| **Third** | Ego/Self | Facing duplicity and arrogance; cultivating humility | ☐ |
| **Fourth** | Abyss | Crossing through fear and flesh; "I am child of Spirit" | ☐ |
| **Fifth** | Awakening | The soul knows itself; world's voice becomes silence | ☐ |
| **Sixth** | Healing | Healing the soul, making it ready for final ascent | ☐ |
| **Seventh** | Illumination | The soul turns to fire; light and goodness | ☐ |
| **Eighth** | Apex | "I AM" — completion, silent restfulness | ☐ |

**Important:**
- **You can only be at ONE bough at a time** (though you may revisit earlier boughs periodically)
- **Honesty over aspiration** — Don't claim Seventh Bough because it sounds good; name where you actually are
- **No judgment** — Being at the First or Second Bough is not "failure"; it's honesty

**Journal prompt:**
*"This week, I am at the [___] Bough. The evidence is: [specific patterns, struggles, or breakthroughs that confirm this]."*

### Step 2: Name the Guardian (3 minutes)

Each bough has a **Guardian** (shadow/power) you must confront. **What is blocking your ascent this week?**

| **Bough** | **Guardian** | **Shadow** | **Active this week?** |
|-----------|--------------|------------|-----------------------|
| **First** | Lord of Wrath | Judgment, rage, condemnation | ☐ |
| **Second** | Voice of Ignorance | Closed-mindedness, intellectual rigidity | ☐ |
| **Third** | Arrogance | "You are not worthy" OR "You are superior" | ☐ |
| **Fourth** | Master of the World (Deceiver) | Fear, attachment to flesh/material, illusion of control | ☐ |
| **Fifth** | (No guardian—awakening itself) | Attachment to the awakening experience | ☐ |
| **Sixth** | (No guardian—healing itself) | Rushing healing, spiritual bypassing | ☐ |
| **Seventh** | (No guardian—illumination itself) | Inflation, thinking you've "arrived" | ☐ |
| **Eighth** | (No guardian—completion) | N/A | ☐ |

**Journal prompt:**
*"The Guardian I'm facing this week is [___]. It shows up as: [specific behavior, thought pattern, or fear]."*

### Step 3: The Apophatic Practice (2 minutes)

**The teaching from Gospel of Mary[^1]:** At each bough, the soul must practice **apophasis** (negation)—denying the Guardian power over you.

**Speak aloud or write:**
> *"[Guardian], I deny you. You have no part in me. I am not [judgment/ignorance/arrogance/fear]. I release my grip on [what this Guardian represents]. I open my hands."*

**This is not affirmation** (positive thinking). **This is kenosis**—active release of the false self structure the Guardian represents.

### Weekly Pattern Recognition

**After 4-8 weeks of weekly check-ins, you'll notice patterns:**

**Spiral pattern** (healthy):
- Week 1: Third Bough (arrogance)
- Week 2: Third Bough (unworthiness)
- Week 3: Fourth Bough (fear at the edge of Abyss)
- Week 4: Back to Third Bough (humility deepening)

**This is normal.** You spiral through the same boughs at deeper levels. Ascent is not linear.

**Stagnation pattern** (warning):
- Week 1-12: All at Second Bough (intellectual knowledge without embodiment)

**If you're stuck at the same bough for 3+ months:**
- You're **avoiding** the work required to move
- Seek external support (therapist, spiritual director, trusted friend)
- Revisit [[Kenosis - The Path of Self-Emptying]]—what are you gripping?

**Bypassing pattern** (red flag):
- Week 1: Third Bough
- Week 2: Seventh Bough (jumped six levels)
- Week 3: Eighth Bough ("I've arrived!")

**This is spiritual inflation.** You cannot skip boughs. If you think you're at Seventh/Eighth Bough but your daily life shows arrogance, reactivity, judgment → **you're lying to yourself.** Return to honest assessment.

## Monthly Soul Work Audit

**Frequency:** First Sunday of each month (or New Moon)
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Method:** Deep journaling, possibly with partner/spiritual director

**The practice:**

### Part 1: Kenosis Depth Check (10 minutes)

**[[Kenosis - The Path of Self-Emptying|Kenosis]] is the core practice of The Magdalene Path. How deeply are you releasing grip?**

**Rate yourself honestly (1-10 scale):**

| **Area of Life** | **Grip Level** | **Evidence** |
|------------------|----------------|--------------|
| **Outcomes** (need for things to go "my way") | 1 (open hand) — 10 (white knuckle) | |
| **Identity** (attachment to roles, achievements, story) | 1 (fluid) — 10 (rigid) | |
| **Relationships** (need for others to validate, complete, or fix me) | 1 (whole) — 10 (codependent) | |
| **Spiritual experiences** (need for visions, insights, consolations) | 1 (detached) — 10 (addicted) | |
| **Material security** (need for money, comfort, safety guarantees) | 1 (trusting) — 10 (hoarding) | |

**Scoring:**
- **5-15 total:** Deep kenosis (rare—you're likely lying or genuinely advanced)
- **16-30 total:** Healthy release with normal human attachment
- **31-45 total:** Moderate grip—work to do, but you're aware
- **46-50 total:** White-knuckle grasping—kenosis is intellectual, not lived

**Journal prompts:**
1. *"The area where I'm gripping hardest this month is: [___]"*
2. *"What I'm afraid will happen if I open my hand: [___]"*
3. *"One thing I can release this month: [___]"*

### Part 2: Singleness Integration Check (10 minutes)

**[[The Magdalene Path#Singleness Ihidaya Inner Wholeness|Singleness]] (ihidaya) is interior wholeness—the integration of masculine/feminine, vertical/horizontal, conscious/unconscious.**

**Ask yourself:**

**☐ Witness Consciousness:** Can I observe my thoughts/emotions without identifying with them?
- If yes: *"I notice anger arising"* (witnessing)
- If no: *"I AM angry"* (identification)

**☐ Masculine/Feminine Balance:** Am I integrating both within?
- **Masculine** (logos): Structure, direction, assertion, penetration
- **Feminine** (eros): Flow, receptivity, surrender, opening
- **Balanced:** I can be strong AND soft, assertive AND receptive
- **Imbalanced:** I overdo one (always asserting, never receiving OR always yielding, never standing)

**☐ Self-Sufficiency vs. Codependency:** Do I seek completion FROM others, or WITH others?
- **Single (whole):** "I am complete; I choose partnership as overflow, not need"
- **Dependent:** "I need them to fill my void, validate me, make me whole"

**☐ Inner Authority:** Can I make decisions from my center, or do I constantly defer?
- **Integrated:** I consult others but decide from MY authority (Strategy & Authority in HD)
- **Fragmented:** I need consensus, approval, or external validation for every choice

**Journal prompt:**
*"The area where I'm most divided (not Single) this month is: [___]. The work is: [___]."*

### Part 3: Relational Health Check (10 minutes, if in partnership)

**If you're practicing the Fifth Way (conscious love in committed partnership), assess the relational dynamic:**

**☐ Substituted Love:** Am I practicing "holding the post" for my partner?
- **Yes:** I stand in their place energetically when they cannot hold themselves
- **No:** I'm rescuing, fixing, or demanding they fix themselves

**☐ Kenosis in Relationship:** Am I releasing control, defensiveness, need to be right?
- **Yes:** I can let conflicts resolve without "winning"
- **No:** I'm still gripping my position, defending my ego

**☐ Transparency:** Are we practicing total honesty (no hidden agendas)?
- **Yes:** I share my inner state, even when it's vulnerable
- **No:** I'm hiding, performing, or withholding

**☐ Sacred Union vs. Codependency:** Is this Bridal Chamber or fusion?
- **Bridal Chamber:** Two whole beings creating a third term (Abler Soul)
- **Codependency:** Two incomplete people merged, losing boundaries

**Journal prompt:**
*"The relational pattern that needs kenosis this month is: [___]."*

### Part 4: [[Contemplative Prayer]] Consistency (5 minutes)

**Track your actual practice, not your aspiration:**

**This month, I practiced contemplative prayer:**
- ☐ Daily (20-40 min) — **Consistent**
- ☐ 4-6 days/week — **Solid**
- ☐ 2-3 days/week — **Spotty**
- ☐ Less than 2 days/week — **Abandoned**

**If you abandoned the practice:**
- **Why?** (Be specific: "I was busy" is ego; "I prioritized other things" is honest)
- **What's the resistance?** (Fear of stillness? Boredom? Lack of immediate results?)
- **What's one small recommitment?** (Even 10 min daily is better than zero)

**If you're consistent:**
- **What fruits are you noticing?** (Patience, spaciousness, compassion, silence)
- **Any obstacles arising?** (Dryness, Dark Night, distractions)
- **Next depth?** (Extend duration? Add second session? Explore Teresa's stages?)

### Part 5: Integration Action (5 minutes)

**Based on this month's audit, what is ONE concrete action?**

**Examples:**
- "I'm gripping outcomes in my business. This month, I'll practice opening my hands after each client session (literal gesture + kenosis prayer)."
- "I'm divided (not Single) in my masculine/feminine balance—I'm all structure, no flow. This month, I'll add one embodiment practice (dance, yoga, walking in nature)."
- "I abandoned contemplative prayer. This month, I'll return to 10 minutes daily, no judgment."

**Write it down. Calendar it. Do it.**

## Quarterly Shadow Scan

**Frequency:** Every 90 days (seasonal shifts: Spring Equinox, Summer Solstice, Autumn Equinox, Winter Solstice)
**Duration:** 1-2 hours (longer if with spiritual director)
**Method:** Deep journaling, possibly in nature or during retreat day

**The practice:**

### Part 1: The Four Shadows of The Magdalene Path (30 minutes)

**[[The Magdalene Path#Kenotic Descent|The Magdalene Path has specific shadow distortions]]. Are you falling into any?**

#### Shadow 1: Spiritual Bypassing

**Definition:** Using spiritual language/practices to **avoid** difficult emotions, conflict, or responsibility

**Check yourself:**
- ☐ Am I using "I'm practicing non-attachment" to avoid commitment?
- ☐ Am I using "everything is perfect" to bypass grief, anger, or pain?
- ☐ Am I using "I forgive" to avoid setting boundaries or confronting harm?
- ☐ Am I using kenosis to **escape** rather than **engage fully THEN release**?

**If yes to any:** You're bypassing. **True kenosis requires full embodiment first.** You cannot let go of what you haven't fully felt.

**Antidote:**
- Name the avoided emotion: *"I'm angry. I'm scared. I'm grieving."*
- **Feel it fully**—body sensations, not just mental story
- **Then** practice kenosis: *"I release my grip on this. I open my hands."*

#### Shadow 2: The Penitent Whore Distortion

**Definition:** Internalizing the patriarchal myth—believing sexuality is "sinful" and must be "redeemed" through suffering

**Check yourself:**
- ☐ Do I believe my body/sexuality is inherently shameful?
- ☐ Am I seeking spiritual validation through purity, martyrdom, or self-denial?
- ☐ Do I view eros as "lower" and agape as "higher"? (This is Gnostic heresy, not Magdalene Path)
- ☐ Am I punishing myself for past sexual choices, relationships, or desires?

**If yes to any:** You've absorbed Pope Gregory's lie[^3] (594 CE conflation of Mary Magdalene with "sinful woman"). **The Magdalene tradition honors eros as pathway to gnosis.**

**Antidote:**
- **Reclaim anointing** — Sexuality is sacred, not shameful
- Read Bourgeault on **A = E × K** (Agape = Eros × Kenosis)[^2]—divine love is not separate from passion; it's passion transformed by self-emptying
- Work with a therapist or somatic practitioner if body shame is deep

#### Shadow 3: Codependency Disguised as Sacred Union

**Definition:** Entering partnership seeking **completion** from the other, calling it "Bridal Chamber" when it's egoic fusion

**Check yourself:**
- ☐ Do I feel incomplete without my partner?
- ☐ Do I lose boundaries when in relationship (fusion)?
- ☐ Am I calling codependency "sacred union" because it sounds spiritual?
- ☐ Did I skip [[The Magdalene Path#Singleness Ihidaya Inner Wholeness|singleness work]] and jump straight to partnership?

**If yes to any:** This is not the Fifth Way. **You cannot enter the Bridal Chamber without first achieving singleness (inner wholeness).**

**Antidote:**
- **Do the singleness work FIRST** (therapy, shadow work, embodiment practices)
- If already in partnership: create space for individual development (separate practices, alone time, therapy)
- Revisit [[The Bridal Chamber and Sacred Union]] — qualification is wholeness, not neediness

#### Shadow 4: Premature Abyss Crossing

**Definition:** Attempting to "cross the Abyss" before completing Lower Astral purification—leading to spiritual inflation or psychosis

**Check yourself:**
- ☐ Am I using psychedelics or intense practices to force transcendence?
- ☐ Am I claiming advanced stages (Seventh/Eighth Bough) while my daily life shows reactivity, judgment, arrogance?
- ☐ Am I skipping shadow work (Lower Astral) because it's "too dark" or "I'm past that"?
- ☐ Do I want the peak experience without the purification?

**If yes to any:** You're bypassing necessary work. **The sequence matters.** Lower Astral (facing the seven demons, dismantling false self) MUST precede the Abyss.

**Antidote:**
- **Return to honesty** about which Bough you're actually at
- **Face the shadow** — Work with a therapist, do IFS (Internal Family Systems), practice kenosis at Third/Fourth Bough
- **Respect the sequence** — Shortcuts bypass transformation, not accelerate it

### Part 2: Open Centers Conditioning Scan (Your Specific HD Mechanics) (20 minutes)

**You have FIVE undefined centers (Head, Ajna, Throat, Heart, Root). Each creates specific Not-Self conditioning patterns that interact with The Magdalene Path.**

**How this affects The Magdalene Path:**

**Kenosis practice for undefined Head:**
- **Release the need to have answers** — "I don't know" is a valid spiritual state
- **Notice whose question you're answering** — Is this MY inquiry or someone else's mental pressure?
- **Let contemplative prayer be EMPTY** — It's not about producing insights; it's about resting in unknowing

**Monthly check:**
*"This quarter, whose mental pressure did I take on as my own? [___]"*

**How this affects The Magdalene Path:**

**Kenosis practice for undefined Ajna:**

- **Sample perspectives without grasping** — It's okay to see multiple truths; you don't have to pick ONE

**Monthly check:**

#### Undefined Throat: Pressure to Initiate/Speak/Manifest

- **Defined Throat:** Consistent access to manifestation energy

**How this affects The Magdalene Path:**

#### Undefined Heart: Pressure to Prove Worth

**Mechanics:**
- The Heart (Will/Ego center) is where **willpower, promises, self-worth** reside

- **Undefined Heart (YOU):** You **amplify** others' willpower, feel pressure to prove worth through achievement, promises, or force

**How this affects The Magdalene Path:**

**Kenosis practice for undefined Heart:**

**Monthly check:**
*"This quarter, where did I practice to prove worth instead of genuine devotion? [___]"*

#### Undefined Root: Pressure to Rush/Complete/Get It Done

**Mechanics:**
- The Root is where **pressure to act** (adrenaline, stress, urgency) is generated

**How this affects The Magdalene Path:**

## Relational Practice Protocols (Fifth Way)

**If you're in committed partnership practicing the Fifth Way:**

These are **ongoing practices** (not timed to weekly/monthly/quarterly), but you can review them during your monthly audit.

### Protocol 1: Daily Kenosis in Relationship (5 minutes)

**Before bed, with your partner (or alone if partner isn't on this path):**

1. **Pause together** (sit facing each other or side by side)
2. **Share one grip from the day:**
   - "Today I gripped [control/outcome/being right/defensiveness] when [specific situation]."
3. **Practice release:**
   - Literally open your hands (palms up)
   - Say aloud: "I release this grip. I open my hands to you and to the Divine."
4. **Witness each other** without fixing, advising, or rescuing
5. **Close with gratitude** (one thing you're grateful for in each other or the day)

**Why this works:** Daily micro-kenosis prevents accumulation of resentment, control, or defensive patterns. You practice vulnerability in real-time.

### Protocol 2: Weekly Substituted Love Practice (15 minutes)

**Once per week (Sunday evening or your designated time):**

**The practice of "laying down your life" (not martyrdom, but **standing in the beloved's place**):**

1. **Partner A shares a burden** (fear, struggle, pain) — 5 minutes
2. **Partner B practices substituted love:**
   - Energetically **stand in their place** (imagine being them, feeling what they feel)
   - **Reflect their light** (hold the truth of their wholeness when they can't see it)
   - **Do NOT fix, advise, or rescue** — Just witness and hold space
3. **Switch** — Partner B shares, Partner A witnesses
4. **Close together:** Brief silence or prayer

**The Abler Soul:** This creates a "third term"—a unified field of consciousness greater than either individual. Over time, this becomes the **Wedding Garment** (resurrection body built through kenotic love).

### Protocol 3: Monthly Bridal Chamber Check-In (30 minutes)

**First Sunday of each month (alongside your personal soul work audit):**

**Sit with your partner and ask:**

**☐ Are we entering as Single beings or seeking completion?**
- **Single (healthy):** "I'm whole; I choose you as overflow"
- **Dependent (unhealthy):** "I need you to fill my void"

**☐ Is this union creating a third term (Abler Soul)?**
- **Yes:** We're more together than we are separately (not fusion, but synergy)
- **No:** We're two individuals coexisting, or we're fused (lost boundaries)

**☐ Is our intimacy sacred or transactional?**
- **Sacred:** Sexual union is sacrament ("This is my body, given for you")
- **Transactional:** Sex is bargaining chip, duty, or escape

**☐ Are we serving the Divine together, or just serving our comfort?**
- **Service:** Our union generates "children of the light" (wisdom, creativity, healing capacity for others)
- **Comfort:** Our relationship is a bubble disconnected from the world

**Based on this check-in, what needs kenosis in our relationship this month?**
