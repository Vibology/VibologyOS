# Astrology Overview

**Pillar 2 of 7** — Planetary timing and cosmic positioning.

## Core Domain

Astrology maps the **Ambient**—the cosmic field at the moment of birth—revealing planetary influences, timing cycles, and evolutionary shifts.

## Key Knowledge Areas

### Fundamental Components
- **[[Planets]]** — The seven classical planets and modern additions
- **[[Signs]]** — The twelve zodiac archetypes
- **[[Houses]]** — The twelve domains of lived experience
- **[[Aspects]]** — Geometric relationships (Sextile, Square, Trine, Opposition)

### Temporal Frameworks
- **[[Transits and Timing]]** — Planetary movement and activation
- **[[Global Cycles]]** — Cross of Planning and collective evolution
- **[[Precession of the Equinoxes]]** — The 26,000-year wobble and aeon shifts

### Evolutionary Context
- **Saturnian Cycle** — 30-year traditional life structure
- **Uranian Cycle** — 84-year modern individuation arc

## Role in [[Vibology]]

Astrology provides the **temporal coordinate**—the "when" and "ambient field" that contextualizes the soul's journey. It reveals the cosmic weather at birth and the evolutionary pressures shaping collective consciousness.
