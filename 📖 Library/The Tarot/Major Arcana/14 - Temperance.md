# 14 - Temperance: Alchemy and the Combination of Opposing Forces

## Archetypal Essence

Temperance is the **alchemical marriage**—the blending of opposites (fire and water, spirit and matter) into the Philosopher's Stone. She pours liquid between two cups, one foot on land, one in water: she operates in the liminal space, the threshold between worlds.

She is [[Diana the Huntress]], the virgin archer who seeks the sacred prey. Temperance teaches that transformation is not violence but *precision*.

## Qabalistic Path

**Path 25: Tiphareth (Beauty) → Yesod (Foundation)**

Temperance descends from the Heart into the Foundation (the etheric body, the realm of image and dream), teaching that spiritual work must be *grounded* in the physical crucible. This is the "Dark Night" prior to adepthood—the testing.

## Hebrew Letter: Samekh (ס)

**Samekh** means "prop" or "support"—the principle that sustains during the ordeal. Temperance is patience: the willingness to *wait* while the work unfolds.

## Astrological Attribution

**[[Sagittarius]]** — The principle of synthesis, the arrow aimed at the distant target. Temperance is Sagittarius as discipline: not the leap, but the *aim*.

## Divinatory Meaning

**Upright:** Combination of forces, alchemy, balance of opposites within the physical vessel.

Temperance appears when integration is required—when opposing drives must be *blended*, not chosen between. She signals: *Neither extreme. The third way.*

## Psychological & Jungian Depth

Temperance represents the **[[Transcendent Function]]**—the psychic process that emerges when opposites are held in tension without premature resolution. This is the birth of the third: the symbol, the synthesis, the new possibility.

Shadow Temperance:
- Over-moderation (bland equilibrium, loss of fire)
- Spiritual bypassing (using "balance" to avoid commitment)
- Perfectionism (endless refinement without release)

## Invitation for Contemplation

What opposites are you trying to *choose between*—and what would emerge if you held both until the third appeared? Temperance does not resolve tension. She *alchemizes* it.
