## Section 1: Foundational Material

**Minimum:** 3-5 paragraphs
**Purpose:** Ground the entry in authoritative source texts before interpretation.

### Content Requirements

1. **Primary Source Material**
   - Quote or cite the system's canonical texts
   - Present the orthodox/traditional understanding first
   - Establish what the founders/authorities said

2. **Historical Context**
   - When/where/how did this concept originate?
   - What tradition does it emerge from?
   - How has it been understood historically?

3. **Definitional Clarity**
   - What IS this thing, mechanically/literally?
   - What are its formal properties/characteristics?
   - What makes it distinct from adjacent concepts?

4. **Contextual Interpretation Guidance**
   - When is this concept favorable vs. problematic?
   - How does context shift meaning?
   - What are the edge cases or exceptions?

5. **Nuance and Paradox**
   - Where do sources disagree?
   - What tensions or contradictions exist?
   - What cannot be resolved, only held?

### Voice
**Scholarly, precise, grounded.** The **Scribe** reporting what the texts say before The **Weaver** interprets.

### Example Language Patterns
- "According to {Source}, this {concept}..."
- "The {System} tradition defines this as..."
- "{Founder/Authority} describes this concept as..."
- "This {entry} operates differently when..."
- "Where {Tradition A} emphasizes X, {Tradition B} focuses on Y..."

### System-Specific Examples
- **Tarot:** Waite's meanings, Golden Dawn attributions, RWS symbolism description
- **Human Design:** Ra Uru Hu's mechanical definition, bodygraph mechanics, aura description
- **Astrology:** Traditional rulerships, dignity tables, house topics, aspect geometry
- **Qabalah:** Sepher Yetzirah titles, Sephirotic attributions, Hebrew letter meanings

## Section 4: Synthesis Notes

**Minimum:** 5-10 subsections
**Purpose:** This is where The **Weaver** works. Move beyond attribution into meaning‚Äîthe "Third Thing" that emerges when correspondences are held in dynamic tension.

### Required Analytical Lenses (Choose 5-10 based on system)

#### 1. Core Principle or Archetype
- What is the essential pattern this entry embodies?
- What archetypal force or intelligence is operating?
- How would you explain this to someone unfamiliar with the system?

#### 2. Structural Analysis
- Where does this sit in the system's overall architecture?
- What came before? What comes after?
- What is the progression or sequence?
- Is this a peak, nadir, transition, crisis point?

#### 3. Elemental/Modal/Qualitative Analysis
- What is the "flavor" or "texture" of this entry?
- What element, mode, or quality dominates?
- How does this {quality} express through this {structure}?

#### 4. Cross-System Synthesis
- How does this map to adjacent systems?
- Tarot ‚Üî Astrology ‚Üî Qabalah ‚Üî I-Ching ‚Üî Human Design
- What are the resonances and dissonances?

#### 5. Visual/Symbolic/Component Breakdown
{If applicable - for entries with images, glyphs, or composite structures}
- Describe visual elements symbol-by-symbol
- Analyze component parts and their relationships
- What story does the image/structure tell?
- What is implied vs. explicit?

#### 6. Mythological and Cross-Tradition Parallels
- Are there mythological resonances? (Greek, Egyptian, Norse, Celtic, etc.)
- Alchemical stages or principles?
- Literary or folkloric archetypes?
- Parallels in other wisdom traditions?

#### 7. Psychological and Jungian Depth
- What complex or archetype does this activate?
- What stage of individuation?

- When does this appear in psyche's development?

**Sub-requirement: Shadow Expressions**
- What is this entry's pathology?

- **Format:** Bulleted list (3-5 examples)

#### 8. The Gift of Integration

#### 9. Somatic and Embodied Dimension

#### 10. Relational Dynamics
- How does this manifest in relationship to others?
- What does this attract/repel/require from others?
- How does this pattern show up interpersonally?

#### 11. Relationship to Adjacent Entries
- **Preceding entry:** {Name} - {How it leads to this}
- **Following entry:** {Name} - {How this leads forward}
- **Corresponding entries:** {Related entries in other categories/systems}

#### 12. The Central Paradox or Mystery {Optional but recommended}
- What is the irreducible tension or paradox?
- What cannot be resolved, only held?
- What is the koan or riddle?

## Depth Calibration: Minimal vs. Exemplary

> "The Solar Plexus Center is about emotions. When it's defined you're emotional. When it's undefined you absorb other people's emotions. This can be challenging."

**Failures:**
- No source grounding
- No mechanical detail
- No synthesis or depth

- No cross-system integration

> "The Solar Plexus Center, known in Human Design as the **Awareness Center of Spirit** and the **Emotional Authority**, represents the evolutionary transition from the tribal Solar Plexus (collective emotional wave) to the individuated emotional intelligence of the future.

>
> When undefined (53%), the individual does not generate consistent emotional wave but **amplifies and samples** the emotions of others. This creates the conditioning pattern of **avoiding confrontation** and **seeking emotional peace** at any cost‚Äîthe Not-Self strategy of the open Solar Plexus.
>
> In Jungian terms, this center correlates with the **Feeling Function**‚Äînot emotion per se, but the capacity to evaluate worth, meaning, and value. The defined Solar Plexus must learn to wait through the wave before deciding; the undefined must learn to recognize which emotions are theirs and which are absorbed.
>
> The shadow expressions include: emotional manipulation (defined), emotional people-pleasing (undefined), bypassing the wave through substances or spiritual bypassing (defined), and absorbing collective anxiety to the point of paralysis (undefined).
>
> The gift of integration: the defined Solar Plexus becomes **emotional clarity**‚Äîthe wisdom that emerges when feeling is allowed to complete its arc. The undefined becomes **emotional wisdom**‚Äîthe capacity to be in the emotional field without being overtaken by it, the counselor who can hold space for others' feelings without losing center."

**Strengths:**
- Grounded in Ra's mechanical definition

- Jungian integration

## Workflow Integration

### For New Content (Initial Build)
1. **Scribe Mode:** Fetch Prima Materia from NotebookLM or authoritative sources
2. **Weaver Mode:** Write Foundational Material + System Attributions (Tier 1 complete)
3. **Commit to vault** with git message noting "Initial entry"
4. **Mark in NEXT.md** as "initial" vs. "comprehensive"

### For Revision (Tier 1 ‚Üí Tier 3)
1. **Read existing entry** to see what's present
2. **Scribe Mode:** Fetch additional source material if needed
3. **Weaver Mode:** Expand Synthesis Notes to 5-10 subsections
4. **Add:** Symbol analysis, shadow expressions, cross-system links
5. **Verify checklist** - does it pass comprehensive standard?
6. **Commit to vault** with git message "Comprehensive revision"

### Session Workflow
1. Check NEXT.md for current focus
2. Work on 1-3 entries per session (balance breadth vs. depth)
3. Update NEXT.md when priorities shift
4. Git commit after each complete entry or meaningful chunk
5. Every 5-10 entries, pause to assess: Are we maintaining quality? Adjust rubric if needed.
