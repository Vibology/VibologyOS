## DAYTIME LIQUID GUIDELINES

### Core Principle

**Liquids only during daylight hours** - this is the fundamental requirement of 6th Color Indirect Light determination. The specific liquids chosen should be determined through Sacral response and Emotional Authority over time.

### Liquid Options to Consider

**Nutritive Liquids:**
- Broths (bone broth, chicken broth, vegetable broth)
- Herbal teas (caffeine-free recommended initially)
- Plain water with minerals (pinch of sea salt)

**Simple Hydration:**
- Plain water
- Sparkling water
- Herbal infusions

### Selection Criteria

**Key Qualities:**
- Minimal to low caloric content (maintains fasting benefits)
- No artificial sweeteners or stimulants (avoid confounding experiment data)
- Satisfying enough to reduce daytime hunger
- Practical for daily consumption
- Aligned with personal preferences and tolerances

### Implementation Strategy

**Start Simple:**
- Choose 1-2 primary daytime liquids initially
- Test tolerance and satisfaction over first week
- Adjust volume and timing based on body feedback
- Add variety only if desired and body responds positively

**Avoid During Initial Testing:**
- Artificial sweeteners (diet drinks, zero-calorie sweeteners)
- Excessive caffeine variations
- Meal replacement shakes
- Any liquids that trigger digestive discomfort

### Finding Your Optimal Liquids

Allow Sacral response to guide choices. What gets an "uh-huh" when you consider drinking it? What actually satisfies during the day? The perfect daytime liquid profile emerges through experimentation, not predetermined rules.
